Materials

Required Materials

This course requires the second edition of “Programming Languages: Application and Interpretation” by Shriram Krishnamurthi. This book is available for free online in both HTML and PDF formats, and need not be purchased, although students may purchase it if they want to do so.

Before each class, be sure to read any assigned chapters of the book, as well as any additional materials included in the syllabus.
